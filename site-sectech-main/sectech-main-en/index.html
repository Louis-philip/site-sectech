About Sectech

The Sectech Industrial Automation and Electrical Solutions was born out of the 20 years of experience of its founders, who possess extensive knowledge in industrial electrical and automation. With a solid background in large multinational companies, they have been involved in projects focused on continuous improvement, refurbishment, and automation of manufacturing equipment, aiming to achieve increased production efficiency and reduced machine breakdowns.
